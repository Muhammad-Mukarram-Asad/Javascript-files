Computer Science (CS)

Computer Science (CS) is the study of the principles and use of computers. Simply put it's the science that deals
with the theory and methods of processing information in digital computers, the design of computer hardware
and software, and the applications of computers. A computer is simply a tool for a computer scientist, like a
telescope for an astronomer.
